The seven Wonders of the World

What are the Seven Wonders?

The 7 Wonders of the World was a campaign that started in 2001 to choose the New Seven Wonders of The world from a selection of 200 existing monuments. The campaign consisted of a poll via Web or telephone and is considered the largest poll on record with over 100 million votes. The seven winners were chosen from 21 candidates based on the original poll and were finally announced on 7 July 2007. Here are the winners:

  • Great Wall of China, China
  • Petra, Jordan
  • Colosseum, Rome
  • Chichén Itzá, Mexico
  • Machu Picchu, Peru
  • Taj Mahal, India
  • Christ the Redeemer, Brazil
